Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela

African hero

Nelson Mandela: icon of reconciliation and forgiveness

Here is a time line of Nelson Mandela's life:

  • 1918: Mandela was born in the village of Mvezo in Umtata, Cape Province - South Africa.
  • 1941: Got his first job as a security guard on a gold mine and then as a legal clerk in the law firm Witkin, Edelman and Sidelsky
  • 1943: He enrolled for an LLB at Wits University
  • 1944: He engaged in politics and got married in the same year
  • 1952: He became the National Volunteer-in-Chief of the Defiance Campaign against apartheid laws.
  • 2013: He died.

"I learned that courage was not the absence of fear, but the triumph over it. The brave man is not he who does not feel afraid, but he who conquers that fear." -- Nelson Mandela
